    One round from take-off to vanish

    Every round follows the same structure. A short betting window opens, players place their stake, and the plane lifts off while the multiplier begins at 1.00x and climbs higher. At a point fixed before the round starts, the plane leaves the screen and the round closes. Anyone who cashes out before that moment keeps their stake multiplied by the visible figure; anyone still waiting loses the stake.

    Because the exit point is locked in advance, no tap during the round can change it. Past results do not influence the next flight either, which is why bonuskong online casino play is best approached as a series of separate rounds, not a pattern to solve.
